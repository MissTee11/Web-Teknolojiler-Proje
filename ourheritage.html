Balancing Rocks Zimbabwe

The balancing rocks, also known as Matopo Hills are located
66.7km away from Marondera. They comprise of igneous rocks weathered into
intriguing shapes. These balancing rocks are located in different parts of the country.
These balancing rocks were declared a national monument in 1994. According
to the National Museum and Monuments website they symbolize the peace and stability of the nation's economy.

Shown below, is the mother and child rock.
There is a rock known as "Domboremari" or the Money Rock. It is printed onto the
Zimbabwean dollar note. It consists of three boulders.
