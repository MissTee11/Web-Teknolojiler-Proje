General Information

Marondera is a small town located in Mashonaland East Zimbabwe,
72 kilometres east of the capital, Harare. It has a small population
of 66200 residence out of the 15 million in the country.
Marondera services a large forestry and farming district and markets timber, tobacco, corn (maize),
beef, and dairy products. It is an educational centre and the site of the Grasslands Research Station.
